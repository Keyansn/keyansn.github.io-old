Line following robot

Overview

For our first year project we were tasked with building a robot powered by a microcontroller of out choice that is able to follow a line, then when breaking from the line it must spiral inwardly until finally coming to a stop. We took a pure digital approach and programmed a PIC using Assembly.

The sensors used were originally regular LDRs but they were affected by natural light sources, which made them inaccurate. Instead we used an infrared emitter and phototransistor sensitive to infra-red.

Due to the design of the wheels, or lack thereof, the robot was rather unpredictable and required a surprising amount of speed to be able to move the bug. It also meant that the bug had a tendency to always turn left which caused issues.
